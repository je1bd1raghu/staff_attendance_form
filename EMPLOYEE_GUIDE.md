# Staff Attendance — Employee Guide

A quick reference for how the attendance system works and what you can and cannot do.

---

## How to mark attendance

1. Open the attendance app on your phone or device.
2. Select your name from the employee list.
3. The app checks your GPS location automatically. The **Check In** button becomes active only when you are within the designated area.
4. Tap **Check In** when you arrive. Tap **Check Out** when you leave.

---

## What is allowed

- **Up to 2 check-ins per day.** If you go off-campus for lunch and return, you may check in a second time after your first check-out. The sequence must always be: Check In → Check Out → Check In → Check Out.
- **Self check-in and check-out** from your own device without requiring admin involvement.
- **Viewing your own attendance records** for the current day in the app.

---

## What is not allowed

- **Checking in outside the designated area.** The system uses GPS to verify you are physically within your duty location (within the permitted radius). The Check In and Check Out buttons remain locked if you are outside this boundary.
- **Checking in without selecting your name.** Both buttons stay disabled until a valid employee is selected.
- **More than 2 check-ins in a single day.** After two complete sessions (in + out), both buttons lock for the rest of the day.
- **Checking out without first checking in.** The Check Out button is only enabled after an active check-in is recorded.
- **Leaving a session open.** If you check in but do not check out, that day is flagged as an incomplete day in the monthly summary. It is counted separately from days with a completed session and does not contribute to your total duty hours.
- **Marking attendance for another employee.** Each device is tracked individually. One device cannot have two different employees checked in at the same time.
- **Using someone else's QR card.** Each QR code is uniquely derived from the employee's ID, name, and designation. QR cards are issued and printed by an admin and carry a printed-on timestamp.

---

## GPS and location

The app continuously watches your GPS position. If you drift outside the permitted radius, the buttons will lock again. You do not need to do anything — the app re-enables them automatically if you move back within range.

The tolerance radius for each location is set by your admin. The app will tell you how far away you are if you are outside the permitted zone.

---

## Incomplete days

If you forget to check out, your attendance for that day is marked **incomplete**. This means:

- The day does **not** count toward your Days Present total.
- It appears as a separate count in the monthly summary under **Incomplete Days**, with the exact dates listed.
- Your duty hours for that session are not calculated.

Contact your admin if you missed a check-out so they can correct the record.

---

## Admin-marked attendance

If you do not have a smartphone or are unable to self-check-in, an admin can scan your personal QR ID card to mark your attendance. Admin-marked records are flagged in the system and include the date and time the QR card was printed.

---

## What admins can see

Admins have access to:

- All attendance records for all employees.
- Daily check-in and check-out times, locations, and GPS coordinates.
- Monthly summaries including days present, incomplete days, total duty hours, and average daily hours.
- The device fingerprint used for each self check-in.

Employee data is stored securely and is not visible to other employees.
